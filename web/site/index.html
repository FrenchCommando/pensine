Pensine

A place for your thoughts.

A fun, visual notes app where ideas float as marbles. Drag, fling, and tap your way through different board types. Lofi vibes, gamified interactions, no account required.

Try it now Testers group Play Store Web TestFlight

Android beta: join the testers group first, then open Play Store (on mobile) or Web (any browser) to install.
iOS beta: tap TestFlight on iPhone/iPad — installs Apple's TestFlight app if needed.
