Pensine — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Summary

Pensine does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data storage

All your boards, workspaces, and settings are stored locally on your device using browser storage (for the web app) or the device filesystem (for native apps). No data is sent to any server.

No accounts

Pensine does not require or support user accounts, login, or registration.

No analytics or tracking

Pensine does not use analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or any third-party tracking services.

No network access

Pensine does not make any network requests. The app works entirely offline.

Export and sharing

When you export a .pensine file, the file is saved to your device or shared via your device's share sheet. Pensine does not upload exported files anywhere.

Children's privacy

Pensine does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.
